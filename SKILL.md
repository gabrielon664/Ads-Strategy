# META Ads Consultation Workflow

## Introduction
This document outlines the workflow for consultations regarding META Ads, aimed to streamline the planning and execution processes for successful ad campaigns.

## Step 1: Initial Consultation
- Discuss client's business objectives.
- Identify target audience.
- Review current ad spend and performance metrics.

## Step 2: Strategy Development
- Propose ad formats and types suitable for the client’s objectives.
- Develop a creative brief aligned with client's vision.

## Step 3: Implementation
- Set up campaigns on META platforms.
- Schedule regular check-ins with the client to report early performance insights.

## Step 4: Optimization
- Analyze performance data post-launch.
- Make necessary adjustments to improve results.

## Conclusion
This workflow serves as a guideline to ensure all essential steps are covered for successful META Ads consultations.
